JOURNEY

Xuan Tai IELTS is a small, deliberate studio for serious learners — bound not by shortcuts, scripted answers, or score-chasing, but by a quiet hunger to write, speak, and think with real precision.

Coach · Founder

I am Xuan Tai, a self-taught IELTS coach. I have skills in writing critique, speaking diagnostics, and band-by-band strategy.

Over the last eight years, I have guided more than 2,400 Vietnamese learners from band 5 plateaus to band 7.5 and beyond — working with university candidates, working professionals, and international school students. Together, we have built a way of preparing for IELTS that is slow, honest, and built to last past the test.
